# 2024: My Year in Review

As 2024 comes to a close, I wanted to take a moment to reflect on what I've learned and built over the past twelve months. It's been a whirlwind of new technologies and challenging projects.

## Shipping Side Projects

This year, I finally launched three side projects that had been sitting in my drafts for years. The key was to stop over-engineering and start shipping MVPs.

1. **A Habit Tracker**: Built with Next.js and Supabase.
2. **A Markdown Editor**: Built with Rust and Tauri.
3. **A Portfolio Template**: Built with Astro.

## Looking Forward

In 2025, I plan to focus more on lower-level languages and systems programming. The web will always be my home, but understanding how things work under the hood is my next big goal.
