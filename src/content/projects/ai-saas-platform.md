# AI Image Generator SaaS

Turn your text prompts into stunning visual art in seconds using state-of-the-art AI models.

## How It Works

1. Enter a detailed prompt.
2. Select your desired style and dimensions.
3. Click generate and download your high-resolution image.

## Business Features

- **Subscription Tiers**: Managed by Stripe, allowing free and premium usage limits.
- **Image Gallery**: Save and manage previously generated images.
- **Credits System**: Buy additional credits on demand.
- **Fast Performance**: Next.js App Router for optimal loading and SEO.
